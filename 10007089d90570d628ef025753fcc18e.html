Nubia Red Magic 3 review: a powerful and smooth smartphone

Smartphone Nubia Red Magic 3 will not leave you indifferent even those who have never used the phone as a device for games. The device contains everything you need for fast operation and easily copes with complex tasks tasks.

Red Magic 3 is a fairly fast smartphone, equipped with a very powerful battery, a large screen and two loud speakers. On the side panel there is a special game mode switch, and inside there is a fan that does not give the device overheat.

Design and features

Nubia Red Magic 3 is really a great phone, even compared to the OnePlus 7 Pro and Galaxy Note 9. Due to the strong the metal structure of the device has a weight of 215 grams. This smartphone is so large that it is quite difficult to use it with one hand, although in games things are quite different.

The back of the Red Magic 3 has an led strip in the middle and recessed in housing elements of red color at the corners. At the top is a pentagonal element for the camera, under which is a hexagon for the fingerprint scanner. The advantage here is also the internal fan, which helps to keep the temperature of the device in the normal range, without giving it overheat.

Because of the slippery black finish, the phone is harder to hold in your hands, which can cause inconvenience. The new Nubia Red Magic received a huge 6.65-inch AMOLED screen at the front, whose frequency reaches 90 Hz. On top of the case is a standard one 3.5 mm audio Jack, USB — C port at the bottom. On the left side there is a "game mode" button and a dock connector. I will dance, which I have not been able to verify in action.

If the phone is turned to a horizontal position, so that its right side is on top, then we we will see the power button, volume control, as well as customizable touch-sensitive side keys and a small cooling vent. The front speakers are placed to the right and left of the huge screen.

Smartphone screen

Gaming phone Nubia Red Magic 3 received the largest and brightest screen, among competitive devices sharpened for games. It is a 6.65-inch AMOLED panel with a resolution of 2340×1080 and a format of 19.5: 9. the Display perfectly conveys any content with smoothness, smoothness, whether it is a video or an exciting FPS game. Even the sunlight will not be a hindrance, everything is here clearly and legibly.

The screen refresh rate is 90 Hz, it is lower than the frequency in the Razer phone-120 Hz, but it is equal to the OnePlus 7 Pro, and it is also higher than almost any other device. Only a small portion of games actually support this feature, so using it will be difficult somewhat limited. Nevertheless, it is great that it is here, it looks amazing. The panel is also very sensitive, with a touch response frequency of 240 Hz, which makes any animation incredibly smooth, including the user interface.

In short, if combine all these features to create a truly beautiful and colorful image that makes games or multimedia an incredible experience. The huge size may be a repulsive factor for some, but it's a big plus for me. Such screens are good not only for games and multimedia, but also for improve the performance of the work. Typing is easier, and multitasking is more useful. If you reduce the display size in settings, you can put a ton of information on the screen.

Red Magic 3 Cameras

When I took Nubia Red Magic 3 out of the box and when I noticed a single camera lens on the back, I assumed it would be the same story as many gaming phones. I also incorrectly assumed that no special efforts of the designers were directed at the camera, in order to direct as many resources as possible to another, more important one place.

Pixel phones have a single lens, and are widely considered the best camera phones on the market. The Red Magic 3 camera isn't as good as the Pixel's, but it's also not mediocre. In fact, the smartphone is equipped with a 48-megapixel Sony IMX586 sensor. I recently tried it in Realme X and Xiaomi Mi 9, and was very impressed with this lens.

Images are sharp. You can zoom in without losing quality, and the resulting frames have good contrast and a pronounced scene. A good addition here is a fairly wide angle and aperture — the latter allows you to achieve good depth-of-field effects even without a depth sensor. A large number of megapixels means that you can zoom in and achieve this from a distance without much data loss, although you should understand what you are doing. There is a Pro mode that gets narrower standard.

The camera's user interface is not very intuitive, and finding what you are looking for can be difficult. However, there are some fun features that are hidden here. Light Draw is a long shutter speed that also records short video. I've always liked this feature on Honor and Huawei phones, and it's nice to see it here as well. The effects of multiple exposures will allow you to overlay images on the layers to achieve some unusual results.

A distinctive feature of the Red Magic 3 camera it supports 8K video recording. The feature is currently in beta, and the camera app recommends using it outdoors. In fact, in 8K, the detail doesn't look very rich, and the frame rate is low. I wouldn't recommend buying a gadget just for the sake of 8K videos unless you have a specific application this. In a phone that already has so much, this is just another reason to brag.

The performance of this lens in low light is not great — again, we see a strange phenomenon where the preview looks much better than the final one result. From a wider aperture and a larger sensor, I expected more. This may be the case in a future update.

The Nubia Red Magic 3 front camera is much less interesting, although it works quite well. This is a 16-megapixel lens that doesn't have any portrait mode, but otherwise produces well-detailed images. But, don't forget to disable beauty mode, which is enabled by default — it does strange things, although it still seems to smooth out my wrinkles.

Software provision

Despite all the power and excess hardware, the Red Magic 3 software is surprisingly low-key. I can only say that this is a good thing. Here we get almost stock Android 9.0. But, I prefer the "Game Space" mode, available via the switch on the side panel. It's basically sends us to a launcher created specifically for games. It will disable distracting notifications (optional) and provide quick, easy access to games, as well as control of features such as the cooling fan.

I really like how easy Game Space is allows you to access your favorite games. This almost psychologically affects how you approach Android games. The fact that the game mode is connected to a physical button makes you take your time and choose the game more carefully. But, I would like to see more opportunities here in the future — perhaps the number of hours played in the game each game, or some social aspect.

However, there are one or two typos in the Nubia Red Magic 3 software, as well as incorrect translations. When I switch to standby mode, the text is in Chinese. These are not fundamental problems, but they can irritate.

Performance and games

The Nubia Red Magic 3 smartphone is able to run modern games as quickly as possible at the highest settings, which can not but please. The latest top-end Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 processor and 8 GB of RAM do a great job at any job, providing a stable frequency frames and preventing hangovers or lags.

Test results:

  • AnTuTu 3DBench: 320 507.
  • Geekbench 4 CPU: 3546 single-core; 11,278 multi-core.
  • 3DMark Sling Shot Extreme: 4 977 (Vulkan).

Red Magic 3 from Nubia copes with a lot of tasks very quickly, whether it's surfing on social networks. difficult or demanding game. This gaming smartphone is quite capable of competing with and bypassing such flagships as the Samsung Galaxy S10, which is also equipped with a Snapdragon 855 processor. The tests that were performed when the game controller was turned off are shown above mode.

The manufacturer itself describes Red Magic 3 as a normal phone on Android 9 Pie. The device, which does not contain viruses, has its own camera application and a changed game mode. A small disadvantage may be a poor translation into Russian language.

Game mode

The game mode switch allows you to quickly navigate to the game library, as well as control the cooling fan speed, see the current device temperature, and system notifications. Red Magic 3 has a Super Performance feature, that allows you to achieve maximum productivity. However, the mode has a disadvantage – fast battery discharge.

The next feature is a 4D Shock mode that adds vibrational feedback. But it doesn't work in all games. In performance mode you can hear an annoying fan sound, but this also depends on how resource-intensive the game is. At high graphics settings, in the game Shadowgun: Legends, after an hour, the phone warmed up noticeably, but there were no problems during the process.

Audio and video

Fortunately, Nubia Red Magic 3 has all two things that an audiophile is looking for on a smartphone: dual front-facing speakers and a 3.5 mm headphone Jack.

The speakers are a little thin, meaning that you don't necessarily get the same performance as the Razer 2. Personally, I feel like there could be a slightly higher amount of bass here, but it's it still sounds very decent. Splitting the stereo can help you better navigate a shootout, as well as when watching your favorite videos.

"4D intelligent vibration" complements the package, ensuring that you will feel more immersed in the gameplay and what is happening around you. Etu a feature we have already seen in several gaming phones. Soon all games should actively start supporting this feature, although at the moment the only ones are PUBG, Knives Out, Asphalt 9 and QQ Speed.

However it is nice to have better tactile feedback when sending or receiving notifications. This little thing I personally appreciated.

The autonomy of a battery

The Nubia Red Magic 3 5000 mAh battery allows you to spend a long time playing online games without denying yourself anything. According to my tests, Red Magic 3 is able to stream videos in Full HD format for almost 13 hours, on maximum brightness. But, another gaming smartphone-Black Shark 2, here was slightly better, lasting 18 minutes longer. In any case, the result is quite good. With the Nubia Red Magic kit, we get an 18W fast charger. The device does not have wireless support chargings.

About Nubia Red Magic 3 price and warranty

At the moment, the price of Red Magic 3 is from 33.5 thousand. for the red or black version with 8 GB of RAM and 128 GB of internal memory. But, on sale there is also a more expensive option, a model with 12 GB of RAM and 256 GB of ROM, the price of which is 49 thousand rubles.

The smartphone is already available in Russia, and you can buy it right now from retailers with a 2-year warranty.

Result

Nubia Red Magic 3 is equipped with everything you need for a comfortable game, has a unique design and high performance. Za 33,000 rubles. we get a powerful phone with a huge screen, great speakers and a dedicated game mode.

Yes, Red Magic 3 is not waterproof, so it is worth keeping it away from water. However, the presence of a modern powerful processor guarantees its relevance for several years forward.

Is it worth buying?

If you like to spend a comfortable time playing games or just need a powerful smartphone, then this device will be a good choice.
