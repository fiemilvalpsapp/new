The review of Asus ZenFone 5 Lite: contradictory and capable

While most people admire Galaxy, Pixel, iPhone and other phones, there are not so popular ones like Asus, which produce good smartphones at a much lower price. Asus Zenfone 5 Lite costs $ 300 (19,800 rubles.) and is located between the middle and high shelves, where today, the competition is extremely high. I have reviewed ZenFone 5 Lite.

Asus hopes to beat its competitors with dual cameras both front and back, a sleek glass back panel, and a fast Zen UI interface. In the past, the company has already I tried to release a high-end smartphone, but the presence of some serious flaws in it deterred users from buying them.

Maybe with Zenfone 5 Lite everything will be different, and it will be able to prove itself as a worthy alternative to the middle class? Let's learn.

Asus ZenFone 5 Lite out of the box

In the box we find:

  • Zenfone 5 Lite Smartphone.
  • The micro-USB cable.
  • Charger adapter.
  • Transparent silicone case.
  • Headphones with extra in-ear plugs.
  • Warranty card.
  • User manual.

The design of the smartphone

Asus ZenFone 5 Lite has a familiar look when compared to previous models of the line. This is the only Zenfone that doesn't have an "eyebrow". The display's side borders are minimal, but the upper and lower ones are significant.

On the top is a 20-MP front-facing camera paired with an 8-MP wide-angle lens and flash. I I think that "brow"would hardly fit into such a combination.

The glass back panel looks nice, but familiar and simple. Adding a red hue revives the look a little. The power and volume buttons are located on the right side and have a nice touch — not too hard and not quite soft.

Big screen

Zenfone 5 Lite features a 6-inch Full HD + display with a resolution of 2160×1080 and a screen-to-body ratio of 80%. The clarity of the display is quite good, as are the colors, however, you can notice a bluish tinge.

Maximum brightness 478 NIT and a minimum of 6 nits, allowing you to see the content in any light.

Interface and functionality

Over the past few years, the Asus Zen UI shell has undergone a number of effective improvements. The interface has become more uniform and less complex intricate. I am a fan of Asus unified user interface and appreciate the level of customization that is offered here.

It has tools for clearing the cache, changing interface themes, managing files, and blocking apps. There are few pre-installed programs on Asus ZenFone 5 Lite. Added improved search across the entire device, which makes the phone's features more accessible.

Processor and performance

Asus has once again proved that middle-end devices are no longer slow. Zenfone 5 Lite is equipped with a Qualcomm processor Snapdragon 630 is paired with 4 GB of RAM, which ensures smooth and uninterrupted operation.

Navigating the interface and solving everyday tasks here does not disappoint. I didn't notice any freezes, apps start and switch very quickly quickly.

Games on Asus ZenFone 5 Lite load more slowly than on high-end smartphones and sometimes there is a noticeable loss of frames, but you can play.

Communication and sound

The Zenfone 5 Lite phone supports simultaneous installation of two SIM cards and one microSD. otherwise, everything is standard here. There is dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.2 and NFC.

The gadget uses an outdated micro-USB, instead of USB-C, but there is a 3.5 mm headphone Jack. The built-in speaker does its job well and offers a clear and loud sound.

Cameras Asus ZenFone 5 Lite

Asus has equipped the ZenFone 5 Lite with an additional 8 MP wide-angle lens, the main 16 MP and front 20 MP cameras. This equipment has cameras for all occasions.

Asus ZenFone 5 Lite does not disappoint with the quality of its pictures. The image is rich and detailed. The edge of objects in the frame is sometimes slightly curved blurry, but overall the focus is excellent.

The exposure is also very good, it creates the right balance between the light and dark parts of the scene. Bright parts of the scene sometimes burn out a little, however, enabling HDR mode completely eliminates these problems, without wasting a lot of time on processing.

The additional 8-MP lens, unlike the main one, does not cope so well with contrasting scenes, which often results in blurry images.

The 20 MP front camera of the ZenFone 5 Lite has the same features it has the same characteristics as the main one, but offers slightly warmer tones. The images are rich and detailed. However, it does not have portrait mode, despite the presence of an additional lens, which is only used to create wide-angle shots up to 120 degrees.

It can be argued that 5 Lite from Asus has some of the best cameras among smartphones in its price range.

Video quality

4K video taken on Asus ZenFone 5 Lite turns out to be well detailed, although the colors are not as accurate as in the pictures. Electronic stabilization helps maintain stability, but it is not enough to completely eliminate it shakes'. The exposure is good, as is the focus time, however, the sound when recording is quite compressed, and does not match the quality of the video.

Calls and quality

During the calls, there were no problems.

Signal strength and audibility on Asus ZenFone 5 Lite excellent. The conversation speaker is loud. At the other end of the line, you can hear clearly and without interruptions.

The Autonomy Of The Asus ZenFone 5 Lite

Zenfone 5 Lite is equipped with a battery with a capacity of 3300 mAh, a full charge of which is enough for the whole day. In the endurance test, the 5 Lite battery has shown a decent 10 hours, which is a good result by modern standards.

Asus ZenFone 5 Lite's charging time is also fast. It takes less than 90 minutes for the battery to be fully charged from 0 to 100%.

Result

Asus ZenFone 5 Lite is a great and affordable phone available phone number. Asus ' commitment to creating a smartphone with strong photographic capabilities continues to impress with results, and thanks to an efficient operating system, this gadget shows good battery life.

The cost of 300$ (19 800 rubles.), implies a potential fight with the more famous competitors. Devices like the Moto G6 are not bad alternatives, but the 5 Lite has a significant advantage in many areas, especially in terms of cameras.

Nokia 6.1 offers almost identical cameras, but worse display quality. Zenfone 5 Lite is one of the best phones that can be purchased for $ 300 (19,800 R.).

Advantages Of Asus ZenFone 5 Lite

  • Great camera.
  • Good performance.
  • Clear and bright display.
  • Updated look and feel.
  • Optimized SOFTWARE.
  • Shooting videos in 4K.
  • Reasonable price.

Disadvantages Of Zenfone 5 Lite

  • There is no USB-C.
  • Audio is compressed when recording a video.
  • The video is not as rich as the photo.
